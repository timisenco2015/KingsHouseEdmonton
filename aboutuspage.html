ABOUT KINGS HOUSE EDMONTON

The Redeemed Christain Church of God, Kings House Edmonton is a bivle believing Church that believes in the saving grace of the Lord Jesus Christ. At Kings House, we strive to bring everyone to the knowledge of God through a dept study of God's Word with simplicity and understanding, giving everyone the opportunity to explore and experience God personally and applying it to every area of life in a practical way

Our Roots

RCCG Kings House is one of the parishes of the Redeemed Christain Church of God (RCCG). RCCG was founded in 1952 abd has grown to become one of the fastest-growing churches in the world, with over 32,000 parishes in over 180 countries. The General Overseer of RCCG is Pastor of Enoch A. Adeboye. Presently, there are established missions in many Africa countries, as well as Europe, Asia, the United States, Caribbean, Canada, South America and Australia. RCCg has also been invloved in establishing schools, rehabilitation centers, childcare and support centers
