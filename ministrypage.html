Ushering Unit

Our Ushering Unit exemplifies the spirit of hospitality that we cherish in our church. They ensure that newcomers feel comfortable and that regular attendees experience a sense of belonging. Their commitment to serving our community is truly inspiring.

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is a vibrant, thriving community within our church family, dedicated to nurturing the faith and personal growth of our young members.

Children Ministry

Our mission in the Children's Ministry is clear: we aim to create a loving and safe environment where our children can learn about God's love, His teachings, and develop a strong foundation of faith. We believe that even our littlest ones can experience the joy and wonder of God's presence.

The Righteous Women

The 'Righteous Women' in our church are a dedicated group of women who are deeply committed to living according to their Christian faith and values. Their mission is clear: to grow spiritually, support one another, and make a positive impact in our church and community.

The kings Men

'The King's Men' is a vibrant and dedicated group of men in our church community who are united by their commitment to living out their faith and values. Their mission is clear: to grow spiritually, strengthen their bonds of brotherhood, and make a positive impact, not just within our church, but also in the broader community.

The Technical/ Media Team

Our church's Technical/Media Team is a dedicated group of volunteers with a mission: to use technology to bring our worship services and events to life. They handle the audiovisual aspects of our gatherings, from sound engineering and lighting to livestreaming and multimedia presentations.
